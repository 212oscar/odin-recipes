Sancocho de Res

Sancocho de Res is a traditional Colombian beef stew featuring tender beef, starchy vegetables like yuca, potatoes, plantains, and corn on the cob. It's a hearty dish prepared in a broth infused with aromatic herbs, offering a delicious and satisfying taste of Colombian culinary tradition.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs beef (chunked)
  • 1 lb yuca (cassava), peeled and chunked
  • 2 ears of corn on the cob, cut into chunks
  • 2 ripe plantains, peeled and chunked
  • 2 large potatoes, peeled and chunked
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1 bunch of fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1 bunch of scallions, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Place beef in a large pot, cover with water, and bring to a boil.
  2. Add onions, garlic, red bell pepper, tomato, cilantro, scallions, and cumin.
  3. Simmer for 1 hour or until beef is almost tender.
  4. Add yuca and continue cooking for 15 minutes.
  5. Add potatoes, plantains, and corn; cook until all vegetables are tender.
  6. Season with salt and pepper, and serve hot.
